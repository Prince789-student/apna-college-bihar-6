UGEAC 2025 Important Highlights

The Bihar Engineering Admission process (UGEAC) is mandatory for students who have qualified JEE Mains 2025 and wish to study in Bihar's Government Engineering Colleges (GECs).

  • Organizing Body: BCECEB (Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board)
  • Exam Required: JEE Mains 2025
  • Participating Colleges: 38 Government Engineering Colleges (GECs)
  • Selection Basis: Merit list prepared by UGEAC based on JEE percentile
