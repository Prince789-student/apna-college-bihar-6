Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025

Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025 is the official admission process for B.Tech seats in Bihar government and private engineering colleges. It is conducted through UGEAC (Unified Guidance Engineering Admission Counselling).

Eligibility: JEE Mains 2025 qualified students from Bihar can apply. Use our UGEAC 2025 College Predictor to check which college you can get before filling choices.

Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025 — Step by Step Process

  1. Registration: Register on the official UGEAC 2025 website with JEE Mains roll number and Bihar domicile details.
  2. Document Verification: Upload required documents — 10th, 12th marksheets, JEE scorecard, category certificate, Bihar domicile.
  3. Choice Filling: Fill college and branch choices in order of preference. Fill as many as possible.
  4. Seat Allotment: Based on your UGEAC rank and choices, seat is allotted in multiple rounds.
  5. Fee Payment: Pay admission fee online to confirm your seat.
  6. Reporting to College: Report to the allotted college with original documents.

Documents Required for Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025

  • JEE Mains 2025 scorecard
  • Class 10 marksheet and certificate
  • Class 12 marksheet and certificate
  • Bihar domicile certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable — OBC/SC/ST/EWS)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Aadhar card
  • Income certificate (for EWS and fee waiver)

Common Mistakes in Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025

  • Not filling enough choices — always fill 30+ choices to avoid getting no seat.
  • Missing document verification deadline — counselling is cancelled if documents are not verified on time.
  • Not checking domicile requirement — Bihar domicile is mandatory for most seats.
  • Ignoring branch options — lower-ranked colleges with CSE can be better than top colleges with Civil/Mechanical.
  • Not using a college predictor before choice filling — leading to poor choice order.

Quick Tips for Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025

  • Use the UGEAC 2025 College Predictor before filling choices to save time.
  • Rank your choices wisely — put dream college first, safe choices last.
  • Keep checking the official UGEAC website for round results and dates.
  • Once a seat is allotted, confirm it immediately to avoid losing it.
  • If not satisfied with allotment, participate in the next UGEAC round (seat floating).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When will Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025 start?

Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025 (UGEAC) usually starts in July-August after JEE Mains results. Check the official website and Apna College Bihar for updates.

Q: Is Bihar domicile required for UGEAC 2025?

Yes. Most seats in Bihar government engineering colleges through UGEAC 2025 require a valid Bihar domicile certificate. Some seats are open for all India candidates.

Q: What is the minimum JEE rank for Bihar engineering colleges?

The minimum JEE Mains rank for Bihar engineering counselling varies by college and branch. The UGEAC 2025 College Predictor on Apna College Bihar can give you an accurate estimate based on 2024 cutoff data.

Q: How many rounds does UGEAC 2025 have?

UGEAC typically conducts 3 to 5 rounds of seat allotment. Candidates can participate in multiple rounds to upgrade their college or branch.

Q: Is there any fee for UGEAC 2025 registration?

Yes, a registration fee is charged during Bihar Engineering Counselling 2025. The exact amount is announced on the official UGEAC website at the time of registration.
