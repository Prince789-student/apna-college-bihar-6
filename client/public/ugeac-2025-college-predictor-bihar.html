Common Mistakes in UGEAC 2025 College Predictor

  • Entering JEE Mains AIR instead of UGEAC rank — they are different. Our tool converts AIR to UGEAC rank automatically.
  • Not selecting the correct category — EBC students get a lower cutoff than UR.
  • Ignoring the Female (RCG) category — Female candidates get extra seats.
  • Only looking at CSE — Civil and Mechanical branches have much lower cutoffs at top colleges.
  • Not checking all rounds — UGEAC has multiple rounds with different cutoffs.

Quick Tips for UGEAC 2025 Counselling

  • Always fill maximum choices in UGEAC choice filling — more choices means better chance of allotment.
  • Prefer branch over college if you want CSE at a lower-ranked college vs Civil at a top college.
  • Keep all original documents ready before counselling date.
  • Check the official UGEAC website for exact round dates and seat matrix.
  • Use this UGEAC 2025 College Predictor to shortlist colleges before choice filling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is UGEAC 2025?

UGEAC stands for Unified Guidance Engineering Admission Counselling. It is the official counselling process for B.Tech admission in Bihar government engineering colleges for the year 2025.

Q: How accurate is the UGEAC 2025 College Predictor?

The UGEAC 2025 College Predictor on Apna College Bihar is based on real official 2024 and 2025 cutoff data. It gives a strong estimate of your expected college and branch.

Q: What is the last date for UGEAC 2025 counselling?

UGEAC 2025 counselling dates are announced by the official Bihar engineering counselling board. Check the official UGEAC website for exact dates. Our platform will be updated as dates are announced.

Q: Can I use my JEE Mains rank in the UGEAC predictor?

Yes. Our UGEAC 2025 College Predictor automatically converts your JEE Mains AIR (All India Rank) to an estimated UGEAC rank using the official rank correlation data.

Q: Which is the best engineering college in Bihar through UGEAC?

BCE Bhagalpur (B.C.E.) and MIT Muzaffarpur are considered the top two government engineering colleges in Bihar based on cutoff ranks and infrastructure. GCE Gaya and DCE Darbhanga are also strong choices.
