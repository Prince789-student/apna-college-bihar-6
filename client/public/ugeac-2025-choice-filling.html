UGEAC 2025 Choice Filling Guide

UGEAC 2025 choice filling is the most important step in Bihar engineering counselling. The choices you fill decide which college and branch you will get. A wrong choice order can cost you a better college.

Before filling choices, use our UGEAC 2025 College Predictor to know exactly which colleges you can get based on your rank.

Check Your Colleges Before Choice Filling

How to Fill Choices in UGEAC 2025 — Step by Step

  1. Login to the official UGEAC 2025 portal with your registration number.
  2. Go to "Choice Filling" section.
  3. Search for colleges and branches you want.
  4. Add them to your choice list in order of preference — best choice first.
  5. Arrange the order carefully. The system will try to give you the highest choice possible.
  6. Add minimum 30-40 choices to be safe. More choices = better safety net.
  7. Save and lock your choices before the deadline.

Best Strategy for UGEAC 2025 Choice Filling

Strategy 1 — If you want a specific branch (e.g., CSE):

Put CSE at all colleges in order: BCE Bhagalpur CSE, MIT Muzaffarpur CSE, GCE Gaya CSE, DCE Darbhanga CSE... then put other branches at good colleges as backup.

Strategy 2 — If you want a specific college (e.g., BCE Bhagalpur):

Put all branches at BCE Bhagalpur first (CSE, ECE, IT, EE, ME, CE), then repeat for the next best college MIT Muzaffarpur, and so on.

Strategy 3 — Balanced approach:

Put dream college + dream branch first. Then dream college + other branches. Then second college + dream branch. Keep going until you have 40+ choices.

Common Mistakes in UGEAC 2025 Choice Filling

  • Filling only 5-10 choices — this risks getting no seat in later rounds.
  • Putting a lower-ranked college above a higher-ranked one by mistake.
  • Not saving/locking choices before the deadline — choices are lost.
  • Not using a predictor to check realistic choices — leads to unrealistic expectations.
  • Ignoring safe choices at lower-ranked colleges — always add safety options.

Quick Tips for UGEAC Choice Filling 2025

  • Use the UGEAC College Predictor to identify reach, likely and safe colleges.
  • Fill 50+ choices if possible — there is no penalty for more choices.
  • Lock choices before deadline — changes are not allowed after locking.
  • Check the seat matrix for available seats in each college and branch.
  • Female candidates must select RCG category seats separately if applicable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many choices can I fill in UGEAC 2025?

There is no strict limit on choices in UGEAC 2025 choice filling. You can fill as many as available. Always fill 30-50+ choices to maximize your chances.

Q: Can I change my choices after locking in UGEAC 2025?

No. Once you lock your choices in UGEAC 2025 choice filling, they cannot be changed. Think carefully before locking.

Q: Which branch is best to choose in UGEAC 2025?

CSE (Computer Science) has the best placement prospects but the highest cutoff. If your rank is not good enough for CSE at a top college, ECE or IT at BCE/MIT can also be a strong choice.

Q: What happens if I don't get any choice in UGEAC Round 1?

If you don't get any seat in UGEAC Round 1, you can participate in Round 2 with the same or updated choices. The process continues for multiple rounds.
