Common Mistakes When Targeting BCE Bhagalpur

  • Only targeting CSE at BCE — ECE and IT are also strong options with slightly higher cutoff availability.
  • Not checking EBC/EWS category — cutoff is significantly lower for reserved categories.
  • Not keeping BCE as a safe choice in lower rounds if not allotted in Round 1.
  • Ignoring the female (RCG) category for female candidates — extra seats available.

Q: What JEE rank is needed for BCE Bhagalpur CSE?

For BCE Bhagalpur CSE in the UR category, you generally need a JEE Mains AIR below 1,00,000 to 1,50,000. The exact UGEAC rank requirement is around 800-1200. Use our college predictor to check your exact chances.

Q: What is the fee for BCE Bhagalpur?

Q: Does BCE Bhagalpur accept direct admission?

No. BCE Bhagalpur only takes admission through the official UGEAC Bihar Engineering Counselling process. There is no direct admission or management quota.
