Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many government engineering colleges are there in Bihar?

There are approximately 27 government engineering colleges in Bihar participating in UGEAC 2025 counselling. The list includes BCE Bhagalpur, MIT Muzaffarpur, GCE Gaya and many more.

Q: Which is the best engineering college in Bihar 2025?

BCE Bhagalpur (Bihar College of Engineering) is the best government engineering college in Bihar in 2025, followed by MIT Muzaffarpur and GCE Gaya.

Q: Are all Bihar engineering colleges affiliated to BEU?

Most Bihar government engineering colleges are affiliated to Bihar Engineering University (BEU). Some are affiliated to AKU (Aryabhatta Knowledge University) or IIIT Bhagalpur.
